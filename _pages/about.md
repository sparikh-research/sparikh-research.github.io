I am an MSc Computer Science student at **Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich (LMU)**. My research is focused on **AI4Science** and **Representation Learning**.

### Research Interests
* **AI4Science & Structural Biology:** Protein language models, algorithmic bioinformatics, and molecular modeling.
* **Representation Learning:** Self-supervised representations, Joint-Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPA), and distillation.
* **Mechanistic Interpretability:** Internal representation probing, latent alignment, and activation steering.
* **Mathematical Foundations & Formal Methods:** Information geometry, learning theory, and interactive theorem proving (Lean 4).

---

### Current Project (MSc Thesis)
* **Hierarchical Predictive World Models for Surgical Video:** Developing spatial and temporal state abstractions within a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA) to predict long-horizon surgical dynamics directly in latent space, avoiding pixel-space reconstruction.

---

### Active Research
* **Research Assistant (Hiwi) — ML for Binary Analysis (LMU Munich):**
  * *Representation Learning:* Designed a cross-modal JEPA mapping compiled assembly code to control flow graphs, and distilled a 1.3B LLM into a 6× smaller target encoder.
  * *Interpretability:* Analyzed cross-modal attention mechanisms and verified local control flow structural preservation in latent space.
