My research focuses on **AI4Science** and **Representation Learning**, specifically within physical, structural, and structured domains.

---

### 1. Hierarchical Predictive World Models for Surgical Video
*MSc Thesis, LMU Munich (April 2026 – Present)*
* **Objective:** Developing temporal and spatial state abstractions to learn predictive dynamics directly in latent space, avoiding pixel-space reconstruction collapse.
* **Focus Areas:** Self-supervised latent predictions, state abstractions, and structured representations for multi-granular video sequences.
* **Project Details:** [Surgical World Models](/projects/surgical-world-models)

---

### 2. Cross-Modal JEPA for Binary Analysis
*Research Assistant (Hiwi), LMU Munich (April 2026 – September 2026)*
* **Objective:** Mapping sequential assembly code to structural control flow graphs (CFGs) using a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA).
* **Focus Areas:** Cross-modal representation learning, LLM encoder distillation (1.3B to 220M), and mechanistic interpretability (probing attention mechanisms and control flow structural preservation in latent space).
* **Project Details:** [Cross-Modal JEPA](/projects/cross-modal-jepa)

* **Lean Formalization:** Verification of the Ford-Fulkerson maximum flow algorithm in Lean 4.
