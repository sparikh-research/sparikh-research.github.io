Proposed a novel graph-theoretic partitioning methodology using the METIS algorithm to address the scaling bottleneck of the AlphaTensor-Quantum compiler. Empirically validated the approach against a Monte Carlo baseline with PyZX, demonstrating up to 35.3% T-count reduction in modular arithmetic circuits by preserving local algebraic structures (gadgets).
